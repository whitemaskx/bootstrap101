Prepare the chicken

Cut the chicken into small pieces and fry them in a pan with a little bit of olive oil.

Prepare the tomato sauce

Chop the onion and garlic and fry them in a pan with a little bit of olive oil. Add the tomato sauce and the basil. Let it cook for a few minutes.

Prepare the cheese

Cut the cheese into small pieces.

Prepare the olive oil

Cut the olive oil into small pieces.

Prepare the basil

Cut the basil into small pieces.

Prepare the onion

Cut the olive oil into small pieces.

Prepare the garlic

Cut the garlic into small pieces.

Assemble the dish

Put the chicken in a bowl, add the tomato sauce, the cheese, the olive oil, the basil, the onion, and the garlic. Mix everything together and enjoy!
